    Idea Generation Template

    Use this framework to structure your team's thinking before you start building.

    1. Problem Statement
    What specific problem are you solving? Who is affected and why does it matter?
    [Your team's answer here]
    2. Target Users
    Who will use your solution? Describe them in one or two sentences.
    [Your team's answer here]
    3. Proposed Solution
    What is your solution? How does it address the problem?
    [Your team's answer here]
    4. How AI is Used
    How does your solution incorporate AI? How does it align with RAISE principles?
    [Your team's answer here]
    5. Expected Impact
    What measurable impact does your solution create for patients or the health system? (Financial, non-financial, or time-saving)
    [Your team's answer here]
    6. Feasibility & Next Steps
    What would it take to implement this? What are the immediate next steps?
    [Your team's answer here]
    Tip: Your answers to this template should directly inform your pitch. Judges will evaluate your solution on desirability, feasibility, viability, and presentation — all covered by this framework.
