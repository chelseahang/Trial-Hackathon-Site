Judging Criteria

Evaluation Process

  1. Judges will review each team's presentation and demo.
  2. Each judge will score projects individually based on the judging criteria.
  3. A link for scoring projects will be provided before the pitch session.
  4. Scores will be compiled, and judges will deliberate to determine winners.
  5. Judges should provide justifications for their selection.
  6. The final decision is made collectively.

Understanding the Criteria

The Value Framework

For the hackathon, solutions will be evaluated on their potential to create meaningful and measurable value across the value framework.

The value framework highlights the values for strategic assessment and decision-making. This structure helps teams evaluate projects with impacts quantified through financial, non-financial, or time-saving measurements.

Responsible AI at Sanofi for Everyone (RAISE)

For the hackathon, solutions will be evaluated on their incorporation of RAISE principles.

RAISE is a governance and accountability framework, with a mission to enable Sanofi to be the first AI-powered biopharma company at scale by mitigating AI-related risks.

Mentor Guidelines

Role of Mentors

Mentors will support participants during the hackathon by providing guidance to coach and challenge teams. Do not build solutions for teams.

How to Support Teams

  • Help teams refine their ideas and clarify their problem statements.
  • Share expertise and provide suggestions.
  • Encourage collaboration and critical thinking.
  • Challenge teams to improve their approach.
  • Offer feedback on their project scope and presentation approach.
  • Guide teams in applying RAISE principles to enhance the responsible use of AI.
  • Assist teams to develop their solution's value proposition using the value framework to quantify business impact.
